## **What is Simpl?**

![Slide 4](screenshots/Screenshot%202026-02-01%20112020.png)

**Simpl is the open-source smart middleware**
that enables **cloud-to-edge federations** and
**all major data initiatives** funded by the **European Commission**

### **Simpl Essentials**

* **open-source** = built-in trust & security, flexibility to deploy, simplicity to customise
* **middleware** = software that lets applications and databases work seamlessly together


### **Cloud-to-edge federations**

Put together resources across cloud and edge computing environments as a cohesive system, creating a seamlessly integrated infrastructure that combines the strength of both cloud and edge computing.


### **All major data initiatives**

In particular the development of **Common European Data Spaces** in a modular and interoperable way.


## **Data space fundamentals influencing Simpl-Open**

![Slide 5](screenshots/Screenshot%202026-02-01%20112040.png)

### The data space context

* Data spaces are **data & service sharing ecosystems**
* Technology and contracts need to work hand-in-hand to enforce proper data sharing
* Simpl-Open brings new features that allow **stronger technology-based enforcement**


### Diagram meaning

* Participants (organisations) connect inside a **Data Space**
* A **Governance Authority** defines rules
* **Simpl-Open helps govern standards for interoperability**
* **Simpl-Open enables seamless interactions between participants**

## **What is Simpl-Live?**
![Slide 10](screenshots/Screenshot%202026-02-01%20112359.png)


**Simpl-Live is the journey of data initiatives towards Simpl-Open adoption.**

Each data initiative follows two phases:

1. **Feasibility Study**
2. **Integration**

### Collaboration with common European data initiatives

Simpl-Live runs in parallel with grant agreements and follows a standard journey:

* Preparatory action
* Deployment action
* Feasibility study
* Integration of Simpl-Open
